== SingleApp  ==

SingleApp is modern style free one page parallax responsive WordPress theme. Inform your visitors all they need to know right from your home page without requiring to go to the other pages. SingleApp can be used for business, portfolio, corporate, agency, photography, freelancers and almost any kind of sites. Get free support at http://themegrill.com/support-forum/ and check the demo at http://demo.themegrill.com/singleapp/
